Name from the package: Watch Later Button.
Summary data from the package: Add a one-click Watch Later button on YouTube and save videos instantly with a 10-second undo.
Description: Save YouTube videos to Watch Later in one click, directly from the video interface.

Watch Later Button adds a dedicated Save to Watch Later control on YouTube so you do not need to open extra menus. When you see a video you want to keep for later, click once and continue watching.

Key features:
- One-click save to Watch Later from the YouTube video page
- 10-second undo after each save in case you clicked by mistake
- Clean, focused UI that stays out of your way
- Lightweight extension built for fast everyday use

Why people use it:
- Removes repeated menu clicks when saving videos
- Makes Watch Later part of your normal viewing flow
- Helps you quickly collect videos to revisit later

Privacy:

This extension does not sell your data and does not use third-party analytics or tracking systems. It works only to perform the Watch Later action on YouTube.
