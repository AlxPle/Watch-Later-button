We've updated our privacy practices to ensure full **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)** compliance. In this post, we explain what GDPR is, why it matters, and how Watch Later Button protects your data.

## What is GDPR?

**GDPR** is a European privacy law that took effect in 2018. It requires websites and services to be transparent about what data they collect, how they use it, and gives users control over their personal information.

Even though GDPR is a European regulation, it applies to any website or service processing data from EU residents. Since our servers are in Germany, we comply with GDPR standards for all users worldwide.

### Key GDPR Principles

GDPR is built on these core principles:

- **Transparency** – We tell you exactly what data we collect
- **Purpose Limitation** – We only use data for stated purposes
- **Data Minimization** – We collect only what we need
- **Integrity and Confidentiality** – We keep your data secure
- **User Rights** – You control your own data

## How Watch Later Button Complies with GDPR

### The Extension Itself

The Watch Later Button **browser extension does not collect any personal data**. Period.

- ❌ No tracking of your browsing history
- ❌ No storage of your Watch Later list
- ❌ No uploads to our servers
- ✅ Everything happens locally in your browser
- ✅ Communication only with YouTube's official services

### Our Website

Our website (watchlater.alxple.com) uses **Umami Analytics**, a privacy-focused analytics tool. Here's what makes it GDPR compliant:

#### Self-Hosted & Under Our Control

Umami runs on our own servers at `uptime.alxple.com`. This means:

- ✅ Your analytics data **never leaves our infrastructure**
- ✅ No third-party companies have access to your data
- ✅ We have complete control over data retention and deletion
- ✅ No integration with advertising networks

#### What Umami Collects

When you visit our website, Umami records:

- Which pages you view
- Your device type (desktop/mobile)
- Browser information
- Approximate location (country level)
- Interaction events (button clicks)

**What it doesn't do:**

- ❌ No individual user tracking
- ❌ No persistent cookies
- ❌ No IP address storage (hashed and discarded)
- ❌ No behavioral profiling
- ❌ No retargeting or advertising

#### Data Retention

Analytics data is automatically deleted after **90 days**. After that period, it's permanently removed from our servers.

#### Your Control

You can opt out of analytics collection:

- **Use Do Not Track (DNT)** – Enable it in your browser settings and Umami will respect it
- **Browser Extensions** – Use privacy-focused extensions to block analytics scripts
- **Contact Us** – Email us if you have concerns about data collection
