Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, the core functionality of saving videos to your Watch Later list is and always will be free.

Does it work on mobile?

This is a browser extension designed for desktop browsers. For mobile, we recommend using the native YouTube app.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We do not have servers that store your data. Everything happens locally in your browser.
